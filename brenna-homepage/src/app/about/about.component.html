About Me!

My education and how I came to choose this major:

I am a senior at Colorado State University studying Computer Science with a minor in Mathematics. While I love the major and am excited to enter the work force, computer science wasn't always my first choice. As a senior in highschool, when COVID was at its peak, I ended up chosing a class schedule with AP Computer Science as an elective. Ironically, I hated the course and told myself I would never do computer science again. As a freshman in college, I came in as a double major in Mathematics and Art. I loved the art classes I took and loved being able to explore different mediums, however I couldn't see a future for myself in that degree. After that I committed to Mathematics and immersed myself in some very interesting and challenging math courses. To meet the requirements for the Mathematics major, I had to take the entry level course to computer science, and since I had already taken the course in highschool, I was able to take the time to dive deeper into the content and get a better understanding of each assignment. After re-taking the entry-level course, I fell in love with computer science and the technical thinking and problem solving that was required for each project. After that I fully committed to the Computer Science major and kept a Mathematics minor. Although I never thought I would've ended up here, I have loved my experience as a Computer Science major and would highly recommend exploring the field.
